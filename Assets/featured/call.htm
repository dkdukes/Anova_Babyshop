Frequently asked questions

Do you deliver?

Yes. We deliver countrywide. You can pay upon delivery if within Nairobi.

Do you have a physical shop?

Yes. We are located at 45 Westlands Rd. Maisonatte 7 . Visit Us Today. Free ample parking available.

If I make an order on the website, how long will it take to be delivered?

Once your order has been confirmed, we offer same day delivery if within Nairobi. Next day delivery is for parcels outside Nairobi.

Is it Free Delivery?

No. Delivery fee is dependent on your location.

Will I get exactly what I order?

Yes. We always make sure what you order is what you get 😊 Should there be a difference in color or print be sure that we shall let you know.

What are the payment methods?

We accept M-pesa, Cash, Credit Card and PayPal.

I just got a gift voucher. How do I redeem it?

Kindly carry the voucher when coming to the shop. If it’s an e-voucher send as an email or Whatsapp 0742219264 for proof of voucher so that we can process your order. All our vouchers have a 6-month validity.

Do you deliver items assembled?

Yes, we can deliver some items assembled upon request. We can also video call for assistance in assembling.
